Slip and Fall

Property owners have a duty to maintain safe premises. When they fail to do so and you're injured, we hold them accountable. We handle all types of premises liability cases throughout Northern California.

  • Parking lot hazards

Dog Bite

California's strict liability law holds dog owners responsible for injuries their pets cause, regardless of the dog's history. Our attorneys help victims recover compensation for medical bills, scarring, emotional trauma, and more.

  • Severe bite injuries
  • Facial scarring
  • Infection complications
  • Child dog bite injuries
  • Emotional trauma
