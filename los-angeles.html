Los Angeles Personal Injury FAQs

Does Frank Penney Injury Law handle injury cases in Los Angeles?

Yes. Los Angeles is one of our California service areas. We represent injury victims throughout California and can meet by phone or video and travel to you when needed, so you do not have to live near one of our offices to work with us.

What types of injury cases do you handle in Los Angeles?

We handle the full range of personal injury matters, including car, truck, and motorcycle accidents, pedestrian and bicycle injuries, slip and falls, dog bites, and wrongful death claims.

How long do I have to file an injury claim in Los Angeles, California?

Under California Code of Civil Procedure § 335.1 you generally have two years from the date of the injury to file a lawsuit, and as little as six months if a government entity is involved. It is best to speak with an attorney as soon as possible.

How much does it cost to hire Frank Penney Injury Law in Los Angeles?

Nothing upfront. We work on a contingency fee, which means you pay no attorney fees unless we recover compensation for you. The initial consultation is always free.
