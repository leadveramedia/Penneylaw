Our Story

Fighting for Maximum Results Since 1995

For over three decades, Frank Penney Injury Law has been the trusted advocate for injury victims across Northern California.

When Frank Penney founded this firm, he had one mission: to ensure that injury victims receive the maximum compensation they deserve, not just what insurance companies want to pay. That commitment has never wavered.

Today, our firm has grown to include multiple talented attorneys and a dedicated support team, but our approach remains the same - treat every client like family and fight relentlessly for their rights.

With over $1 billion recovered for our clients and offices across Northern California, we have the resources and experience to handle cases of any size and complexity.
